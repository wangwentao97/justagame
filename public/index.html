How Many Points Can You Get?

This is a game based on game theory.

Game theory studies interactive decision-making, where the outcome for each participant depends on the actions of all.

  • There are several questions in this game.
  • Each question has some options.
  • You will earn or lose points by choosing the options.

*Remember: the points you earn or lose are also affected by others' decision. When choosing your "strategy", you should take into account the choices of others.

Good Luck.
